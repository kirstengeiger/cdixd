In this project we were tasked to use a variety of vector shapes
and trending type treatment to lay out web design. We were also
meant to assign different classes to different sets of SVGs and
design different hover states for them.

I decided to incorporate this into a guessing game of which mystery
fruits were in season. This involved hovering over the illustrated SVGs
to discover the answer.
