Best Fruits in Season

Fruits are one of my favourite things to eat all year round but they're even better when you know what to get in season! So next time you make a stop to your grocery store or farmer's market, maybe keep this in mind to make that treat just a little bit more special.

Spring

  • Cherries

  • Kiwis

Summer

  • Mangoes

  • Watermelons

Fall

  • Apples

  • Pears

Winter

  • Persimmons

  • Pomegranates

Spring

  • Cherries

    • These are such a tart treat in the spring, a sign that the first cold has gone!

  • Kiwis

    • Kiwis and especially Golden Kiwis are as just as fun to eat as they are good. Especially if you can get everything out of the skin in one go!

Summer

  • Mangoes

    • There is nothing better than a perfectly sweet and ripe mango on a summer day.

  • Watermelons

    • Slices of some cold watermelon when warm is perfectly refreshing or even in a nice shake.

Fall

  • Apples

    • The perfect fruit to make warm tartes and pies with as it gets colder.

  • Pears

    • Biting into a nice crisp pear slice really feels like such a treat in the fall.

Winter

  • Persimmons

    • Such a beautifully coloured fruit and almost melts in your mouth.

  • Pomegranates

    • Half the fun of this fruit is picking the little pieces out to munch on. Just be careful to not get stained!
